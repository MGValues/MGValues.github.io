MGValues

What is MGValues?

MGValues is a political compass test that is based on the PA-EP Mock Government. It is a fork of the SapplyValues Github repository. You will be presented by a statement, and then you will answer with your opinion on the statement, from Strongly Agree to Strongly Disagree, with each answer slightly affecting your scores. At the end of the quiz, your answers will be displayed on a political compass.

There are questions in the test.

Universal Compass

If you are a member of the Mock Government, feel free to DM a Pyro#1716 to share your score. It will be added to a universal compass that can be seen in the official Board of Information channel. The Board of Information may reach out to you if you are a prominant member of the Mock Government to ask for your score.

Your questions / results suck!

The Board of Information is in charge of the creation of this test. Please contact Pyro#1716 if you have a complaint/question.

Privacy notice

At the end of the test, you will be given the option to fill out information about how you describe yourself politcally. There will be 3 options. The first will take you to a small survey and will record your answers to that along with the results. The second will just record your results. The third will not record anything. All information recorded is totally anonymous, and is being used for a research project by the creator of AltValues.
